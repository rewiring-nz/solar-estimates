### Molesworth Station Dataset

#### Molesworth Station is:
* A rural cluster of 30ish buildings,
* Surrounded by empty rural country,
* Shaddowed from the north by the Inland Kaikoura Range and Mount Chisholm,
* In Marlborough high country in New Zealand's South Island.

This small dataset is useful for testing mountain shading.

Datset Details:
* 3 x 3 km
* ~ 35 MB
* Building Outlines
* 1m Digital Surface Model
* 8m Digital Elevation Model

Spatial Extent:
* X (Easting): 1619700 to 1622700
* Y (Northing): 5340500 to 5343500
* CRS: NZTM2000 / EPSG:2193
